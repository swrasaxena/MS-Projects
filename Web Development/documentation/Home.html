Home Page Design


My home page has mainly two components:

1. Responsive Image Slider - I have taken this image slider from Pixedelic.com which has a camera basic slider with all random image slide effects. I have used static images as part of this slider since my API did not have much images and other reason being I could tailor the images as per my need.
Images courtesy - Google and NFL.com

2. Audio - I have used HTML5's <audio tag> to play NFL's theme song. I have included the audio controls to play or pause it as and when needed.
